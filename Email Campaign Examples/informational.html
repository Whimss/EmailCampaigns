TNR May Be the Solution!

What is TNR?

Trap-Neuter-Release

Trap-Neuter-Release (TNR) is the humane community-based program approach to controlling feral cat overpopulation.

What are the benefits?

  • Prevents infections and breast tumors.
  • Early spaying offers best protection.
  • Prevents cancer and prostate issues.
  • Spayed females won’t go into heat.
  • Males less likely to escape to find mates.
  • Males less likely to spray urine to mark territory.
  • Early neutering can prevent aggression.
  • Spaying/neutering is cost-effective.
